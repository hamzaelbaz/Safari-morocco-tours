7-Day Toubkal Ascent & Chegaga Desert Tour from Marrakech

Experience the ultimate Moroccan adventure on this 7-day journey that takes you from the towering heights of Mount Toubkal to the sweeping dunes of the Sahara’s Erg Chegaga. This immersive tour combines mountain trekking, desert exploration, and authentic encounters with Berber and Sahrawi nomadic cultures.

From the High Atlas Peaks to the Heart of the Sahara

Climb North Africa’s highest peak and discover the mesmerizing landscapes of southern Morocco, from lush valleys to starlit desert bivouacs.

Starting from Marrakech, this active itinerary blends physical challenge with rich cultural immersion. After a thrilling 3-day Toubkal ascent, continue south through the dramatic Draa Valley to explore the majestic dunes of Chegaga by camel and 4x4.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Depart Marrakech and drive to Imlil. Begin the Toubkal approach trek and overnight at the Toubkal Refuge or nearby bivouac (3,200m). (6 hours total / +1,300m elevation gain)

Day 2: Early morning summit of Mount Toubkal (4,167m) with panoramic views of the Atlas and Sahara. Return to refuge for overnight. (5.5 hours / +/-960m)

Day 3: Descend to Imlil and transfer to Ouarzazate via Tizi N'Tichka Pass, with visits to iconic sites like Aït Ben Haddou. Overnight in an ecolodge. (3–4 hours trek + 4 hours drive)

Day 4: Journey through the Draa Valley to Mhamid El Ghizlane, gateway to the Sahara. Overnight in a fixed desert camp with traditional comforts and optional hammam. (4 hours drive)

Day 5: Camel caravan trek to Erg Lbour. After lunch, transfer by 4x4 to the towering dunes of Erg Chegaga. Overnight in nomadic bivouac under the stars. (4 hours walk + 50 km off-road drive)

Day 6: Free day in the desert. Lunch near a sacred oasis or with a nomadic family. Optional hike or camel ride to the highest dune at sunset. Another night under the stars in Chegaga.

Day 7: Morning departure through Iriki Lake and Foum Zguid, visiting Tazenakht and Aït Ben Haddou en route back to Marrakech. (Approx. 8 hours drive)

Why Choose This Adventure?

  • ✔️ Summit Mount Toubkal, North Africa’s highest peak
  • ✔️ Sleep under the stars in Sahara desert bivouacs
  • ✔️ Experience the diversity of Morocco’s mountains and dunes in one trip
  • ✔️ Immerse in authentic Berber and nomadic Sahrawi culture
  • ✔️ Ideal from April to October for active travelers age 13+

What’s Included

This 7-day tour includes all private transport, English-speaking guides, mule and camel support, lodging in refuges, lodges, and desert camps, plus meals and bottled water. Vegetarian meals available on request.

Basic mountaineering gear is recommended for the Toubkal ascent: high mountain boots, warm layers, windbreaker, hat, gloves (except in July–August). Trekking level is intermediate to sporty. Luggage is carried by mules in the mountains and camels in the desert.

Private Departures & Custom Options

Private tours available for groups of 2 or more. Toubkal ascent can be done in 2 or 3 days depending on fitness level. In spring and autumn, the desert portion (days 5–6) can be replaced with a 2-day nomadic trek across the Draa Valley’s dunes.
